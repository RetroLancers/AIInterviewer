# How We Do Tables

## Table Placement
1.  **Locations**: Tables go in both:
    *   `@[AIInterviewer/Migrations]`
    *   `Servicemodel\Tables`
2.  **Organization**:
    *   Above that, they are grouped in folders with similar domains.
    *   Example: `AiInterviewer.ServiceModel\Tables\AI\AIChatLogHistory\AIChatLogHistory.cs`
